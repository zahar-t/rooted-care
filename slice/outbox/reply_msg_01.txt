Hey Sofia! Great question — and honestly, good news. On an otherwise happy monstera that's pushing out a new leaf, a couple of yellow leaves right at the bottom is usually nothing to panic about: it's often just the plant shedding its oldest leaves. That said, yellowing can point to overwatering, which monsteras really don't love.

Here's the one thing to try: instead of a little every few days, wait until the top 5cm of soil feels dry, then water thoroughly and let it drain fully. That usually sorts it out.
